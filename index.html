Building Info

Building & Amenities

Elevator Access

The elevator is located to the right of the main lobby. Your key fob grants access to floor 4. Press your floor and wait — elevator takes approx. 30 seconds. Stairs available through the left door in case of emergency.

Parking

One assigned parking spot is included — Space #12 in the underground garage. Access via the ramp on Calle Evaristo Morales. Use the same key fob for the parking gate. Visitor parking is available on the street.

Trash Disposal

Trash chute is on every floor near the elevator. Regular trash goes in the black bins. Recyclables in the blue bins on the ground floor. Bulk items: contact building admin. Collection is Monday, Wednesday, and Friday.

Building Admin

The building concierge is available Monday to Saturday, 8am–8pm, in the lobby. For after-hours emergencies, call the building's 24/7 security line. They can assist with lost keys, package delivery, and noise complaints.

Guidelines

House Rules

We trust our guests. These simple guidelines help preserve the experience for everyone.

No Smoking Smoke-free property including balcony. Violation incurs deep-cleaning fee.
Respect Neighbors Quiet hours: 10pm–8am. Keep music at conversational volume indoors.
A/C Off When Away Please switch off A/C whenever leaving the apartment for your comfort and to conserve energy.
Care for Furnishings Treat furniture, artwork, and appliances as your own. Report any damage immediately.
Guests & Visitors Maximum overnight guests as per booking. Day visitors welcome but must leave by 10pm.
Check-Out Protocol Check-out by 11am. Dishes washed, trash bagged, keys left on the kitchen counter.
